It's with a heavy heart that I'm sharing the news that Fruitful will be closing down permanently effective March 31, 2021. Our website, app, forum and social media channels will all be taken down at this time.

It is never an easy decision to close a business, especially when its mission is so personal and purposeful. However, we faced an incredibly challenging year and costs associated with operating and maintaining the platform have continued to outpace our revenue projections.

While this was a painful decision, we are proud of the impact we have made on real lives and families. At the height of the company, Fruitful had four employees, over 5,200 users and notable partnerships with trusted healthcare companies and fertility brands.
